About Me

My process begins with inspiration drawn from various architectural styles, combined with a personal artistic touch. As an architect with a great love for drawing and design, I found myself constantly creating unique pieces that reflected my passion for both fields.

Friends and family encouraged me to share my work with a wider audience, which led to the creation of this Etsy shop. Every item you find here is a labor of love, crafted with meticulous attention to detail. From custom pieces to curated collections, I strive to bring a touch of architectural artistry to each creation. I believe in transparency and open communication. If you have any questions or special requests, please feel free to reach out. I am here to ensure your experience with OMCRAFSTYLE is nothing short of exceptional.

Thank you for being a part of this journey with me. Your support allows me to continue doing what I love, and I can't wait to see how my creations find a place in your world.
